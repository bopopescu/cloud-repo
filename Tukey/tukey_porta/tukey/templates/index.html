What is the OSDC?

We provide and support cloud computing and storage services for the scientific research community. The OSDC is run by the Open Cloud Consortium, a non-profit organization whose primary goal is to support scientific advances by working with researchers in a variety of disciplines.

Why use the OSDC?

Our cloud services are based on the principles of openness and interoperability. The OSDC infrastructure is tailored towards the high performance storage and compute resources often required for scientific discovery. We view the OSDC as complementary to commercial cloud services available.

How do I get started?

First, apply for an account. Once you have an account, you can take a look at the OSDC instructions, login to the console and get started.
